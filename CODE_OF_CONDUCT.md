# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place to collaborate on security research
methodology. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps the project:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and critique the work, not the person.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keep the focus on the methodology and the public-visibility contract.

Behavior that has no place here:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Committing live third-party target data in violation of the contribution
  contract.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces and when an individual
represents the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior to the maintainers
at `nuclide-research.com`. We review every report and respond as we judge
appropriate to the circumstances. We keep the reporter's identity confidential.
